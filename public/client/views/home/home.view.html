Introduction

This website is for form building. users could create and modify their own forms and fields in the Form page. Creating and editing the fields for a form is highly user-interactive in this website. Users could add, edit, clone, delete and drag the fields to change the order. The philosophy of design is mobile first and it's also a single page application.
